Objectives of Bangladesh Education Forum

Bangladesh Education Forum 2022 aims to bring interested Bangladeshi universities, and engineering and medical colleges closer to the international high-school students in the UAE and GCC who would be looking to enrol for universities in different countries, in 2023 and beyond through their participation at the exhibition and conference.

Bangladesh Education Forum 2022 Business Activities

  • Conference: Presentations; Panel Discussions; B-2-B Engagement
  • Exhibition: Display of products and services by Bangladeshi Institutions
  • Meetings: Meeting with UAE-based students and education consultants
  • Seminars: Business Seminars on specific educational areas
  • Match-Making: Meeting between Bangladeshi universities and students
  • Site Visits: Visit to Premises of Selective Schools/Colleges
  • Excursion Tour: Dubai-Khorfakkan-Fujairah-Dubai Tour including Lunch
  • Desert Safari: Desert Safari, Dune Bashing, Camel Riding & Barbecue Dinner
  • Dinner Cruise: A Three-Hour Dinner Cruise for interested participants
